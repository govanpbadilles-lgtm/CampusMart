Frequently Asked Questions
How do I register as a seller? ▾
Register an account and select "Campus Seller" during sign-up. Upload your school-issued permit and wait for admin approval (usually within 24 hours).
My order is stuck on "Preparing". ▾
Contact the seller directly at their listed pickup location, or report the issue via this form and include your Order ID.
Is CampusMart free to use? ▾
Yes! CampusMart is completely free for both buyers and sellers. No platform fees or commissions are charged.
Can non-students use the platform? ▾
Currently, CampusMart is exclusive to enrolled students and faculty with valid university IDs. This keeps the marketplace secure.
