About CampusMart

Built for Students,
By Students

CampusMart is your university's all-in-one campus marketplace — designed to make ordering uniforms, meals, supplies, and books faster and more convenient than ever.

Our Mission

Connecting campus communities through seamless commerce

🎓
Student-First Design

Every feature was designed around real student needs — skip lines, order ahead, and manage everything from your phone.

🔒
Secure & Verified

Only registered students and faculty with valid IDs can access the platform. All sellers are admin-approved before listing.

🌱
Empowering Campus Sellers

We help student entrepreneurs grow their small businesses within the campus community with zero platform fees.

⚡
Fast & Reliable

Real-time order updates, push notifications, and a streamlined checkout process mean less waiting and more doing.

🤝
Community-Driven

Features are built based on student feedback. Rate sellers, suggest products, and help shape the platform you use every day.

📱
Mobile-First

Designed for smartphones first. Browse, order, and track everything on the go — whether you're between classes or at the dorm.
